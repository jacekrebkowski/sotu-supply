02 — Four Themes

Where Supply focuses,
how teams ladder up

Each theme is defined by a single, measurable question: does this help more new and casual listers get to a sale within 7 days?

01
Theme 01
Make Selling Obvious
Create one simple baseline selling experience that helps every seller understand how to get from intent to sale. Kill seller onboarding — sellers share info while they learn selling tactics. We ask for info at the right time.

"List an item, make it sale-ready, price it well, take the next best action."

Do this

  • Priority: new sellers first
  • Find the clearest new seller a-ha moment (no onboarding)
  • Activation while exploring the app
  • Define the simplest universal baseline
  • Demand alignment on ranking/discovery of new lister inventory

What to avoid

  • First listing volume without sale likelihood
  • Too much education before value
  • Onboarding that doesn't ladder to STR
02
Theme 02
Make Every Item Sale-Ready
Improve the quality, accuracy, trust and discoverability of every listing without adding unnecessary seller effort. Covers photos, attributes, condition, size and fit, description, item accuracy, trust signals and structured data for search, recommendations and future AI discovery.

Listing Team can now predict all attributes. The opportunity is automation over friction.

Do this

  • Define "sale-ready" in a measurable way
  • Identify proven quality levers vs what needs evidence
  • What can Depop infer, enrich or automate?
  • Electric feeling when completing a listing
  • Make listing human and exciting

What to avoid

  • Defining quality as "more fields completed"
  • Slowing listing creation without upside
  • Asking sellers for info Depop can infer
03
Theme 03
Price for Demand
Make the right price and buyer opportunity obvious so sellers aren't guessing and buyers immediately understand the value. Combines pricing, buyer-side price perception, offers, demand matching, search, recommendations and market signals.

Pricing is the highest-confidence STR lever — operationalise it first. Collab with Demand.

Do this

  • Pricing as the first lever we operationalise
  • Connect pricing to buyer-side value perception
  • Show sellers what they make vs what buyers see
  • Demand's role matching new listings to buyers
  • Coach vs automate — know when each applies

What to avoid

  • Generic pricing advice
  • Separating price from buyer demand
  • Manual actions when we know the right answer
  • Optimising only for seller margin
04
Theme 04
Sell Until It Sells
Turn the post-listing journey into an active conversion engine using feedback, next best actions and automation. Covers Depop as coach, post-listing actions, seller tooling, feedback loops, offers, price drops, relisting, boosting and auto-negotiate.

Sellers currently list and wait. The first 7 days after listing need to become the most active optimisation window.

Do this

  • Hierarchy of post-listing actions by STR impact
  • Tell sellers when a listing isn't selling after 7 days
  • Automate what's known, get consent for the rest
  • Clever post-listing actions

What to avoid

  • Noisy modules with unclear STR impact
  • Too many tools, not enough guidance
  • Coaching when the answer is already known
  • Sellers feeling like they're shouting into the void
03 — Big Unknowns

The questions we
need to answer first

01
Measurement
Are we aligning on new lister STR, new listing STR, or both? This shapes everything we build.
02
Time window
Is 7-day STR the ambition and 14-day STR the operating metric? 7 days is the bold choice.
03
Ownership
Which themes sit clearly in Supply, and which need Demand or Marketplace partnership to move?
04
Evidence
Pricing is proven. Which listing quality and post-listing levers actually move STR? We need data.
05
UX principle
When do we guide, when do we automate, and when do we stay out of the way entirely?
